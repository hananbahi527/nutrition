Food Waste

The problem of food waste is a global problem facing all societies, resulting in a lot of damage and causing many other problems that cause double damage

According to the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), about 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted each year of the total amount of food produced in the world It estimates that if food waste was reduced by 25%, enough food could be saved to feed All people who suffer from hunger around the world

Healthy Food

Eating a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables can boost your overall health.

Healthy food is crucial because it provides essential nutrients that support bodily functions and prevent chronic diseases.

Consuming a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins is vital for maintaining overall health. These foods are packed with essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that help prevent chronic diseases, improve energy levels, and support a strong immune system. For instance, the fiber in whole grains aids digestion, while antioxidants in berries can reduce inflammation. A balanced diet not only enhances physical well-being but also supports mental health, contributing to a higher quality of life.
